About Ask Coco

Our Mission

At Ask Coco, we're revolutionizing college counseling through AI-powered personalization. Our mission is to make expert college guidance accessible to every high school student, ensuring they make informed decisions about their academic future.

Our Story

Founded by education and technology experts, Ask Coco emerged from a simple observation: quality college counseling shouldn't be a privilege, but a right. We've combined artificial intelligence with expert knowledge to create a platform that provides personalized guidance at scale.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Making expert college guidance available to all students
  • Innovation: Leveraging AI technology to provide personalized support
  • Excellence: Maintaining high standards in education guidance
  • Empowerment: Helping students make informed decisions about their future

Our Approach

We combine artificial intelligence with expert knowledge to provide:

  • Personalized college recommendations
  • Custom academic planning
  • Financial aid guidance
  • Application strategy support
  • Real-time progress tracking

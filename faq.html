Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ask Coco?

Ask Coco is an AI-powered college counseling platform that provides personalized guidance to high school students throughout their college preparation and selection journey.

How does the AI counseling work?

Our AI analyzes your academic profile, interests, and preferences to provide personalized college recommendations, academic planning, and application strategies. It learns from your interactions to offer increasingly tailored advice.

Is Ask Coco free to use?

Ask Coco offers both free and premium features. Basic college search and general guidance are free, while personalized counseling and advanced features are available through our premium plans.

How accurate are the college matches?

Our college matching algorithm considers multiple factors including academic performance, location preferences, financial considerations, and career goals to provide highly accurate recommendations.

Can parents access their student's account?

Yes, we offer parent access features that allow parents to view their student's progress while maintaining student privacy for personal communications.

How do you protect student data?

We take data privacy seriously and comply with all relevant education privacy laws. All personal information is encrypted and securely stored. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What grade should I start using Ask Coco?

While you can start at any time, we recommend beginning in 9th or 10th grade to maximize the benefits of our academic planning and college preparation guidance.

Do you offer human counselor support?

Yes, our premium plans include access to human college counselors who can provide additional guidance and review AI recommendations.
